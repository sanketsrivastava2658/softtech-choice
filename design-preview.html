Typography

Three voices. Zero Inter.

Grotesk for confidence, Geist for legibility, mono for every number. The mono numerals are the signature.

Cabinet Grotesk · 800 Display / headings / page titles
Meetings booked, not emails sent.
Geist · 400–600 Body / UI / labels / buttons

Clients judge the entire engagement by the dashboard they log into. The interface has to read as serious software the moment it loads — clear hierarchy, honest numbers, no decoration competing with the data. Geist holds up at 12px in dense tables and still feels modern at 16px in prose.
